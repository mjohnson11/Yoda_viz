Yeast antibody display project

Using sort-seq technologies, we measured the Kd of a set of antibody genotypes, which are combinations of 16 mutations that separate a germline genotype from a somatic genotype. The graph here shows a mapping of these points in 2D space based on both genotype similarity and phenotype (Kd) similarity. You can hover and click-and-drag on the graph to see genotypes (and their corresponding phenotypes in the plot at right), and you can click on mutations on the left to lock them or color the points based on the alleles at those genotypes.
