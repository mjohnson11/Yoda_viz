Yeast antibody display project

Using Tite-Seq, we measured the KD (equilibrium binding dissociation constant) of a combinatorial library based on an anti-influenza broadly neutralizing antibody, CR-6261, to two distinct influenza antigens. This library contains all possible combinations of 11 mutations present in the somatic CR-6261 sequence. The KD for each antigen were measured in biological triplicate. Here the average KD are displayed in a force-directed graph, where each variant (node) is connected to its 11 single-mutation neighbors, with edge weight inversely proportional to change in binding affinity to H1 (edges not shown for clarity) such that variants with similar genotypes and KD will tend to cluster.
